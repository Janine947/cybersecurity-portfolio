# Lab Notes

## Lab 1 - Basic Port Scanning
### Objective
Understand how port scanning works.

### Tool Used
Nmap

### Activity
Ran a basic scan on a test machine in a controlled lab environment.

### Findings
- Open ports can reveal available services
- Exposed services may increase the attack surface

### Lesson Learned
Only scan systems you own or are authorized to test.

## Lab 2 - Packet Analysis
### Objective
Learn how to inspect traffic using Wireshark.

### Tool Used
Wireshark

### Activity
Captured sample traffic and reviewed protocols such as HTTP and DNS.

### Findings
- Unencrypted traffic can expose sensitive information
- Protocol analysis helps identify suspicious behavior

### Lesson Learned
Encrypted communication is important for protecting sensitive data.
